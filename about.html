About

Keith and Ricky Carter

Family-run since 1984. When you call AKC, they're who shows up.

Keith Carter has been an electrician in the Valley for over forty years. He started AKC Electric in 1984, and today he runs it with his son Ricky.

The work spans the whole range: full electrical for new construction, rewiring through remodels, and the ordinary repairs that keep a house running — a dead outlet, a light that quit, a room that never had enough of them. Builders call for the first. Homeowners call for the last. It's the same two people either way.

That's the difference worth knowing: when you call AKC, Keith and Ricky are who show up. Not a rotating crew, not a subcontractor. It's why most of their work comes from people who've used them before, or from builders who keep calling back.

AKC Electric is licensed by the Arizona Registrar of Contractors under ROC 278945, and is bonded and insured. It has been a BBB Accredited Business since 2012 and holds an A+ rating.
